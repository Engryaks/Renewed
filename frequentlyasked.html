Programs & Courses

Information about our training programs and courses

What types of programs do you offer?

We offer a wide range of agricultural programs including:

  • Poultry & Livestock Training
  • Crop & Agricultural Training
  • Agricultural Business & Technical Skills
  • Renewable Energy Training
  • Professional Development & Extension

Do you offer certification for your programs?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our programs. Some programs also offer industry-recognized certifications that can enhance your career prospects.

Can I enroll in multiple programs at once?

Yes, you can enroll in multiple programs simultaneously. However, we recommend discussing your study plan with our academic advisors to ensure you can manage the workload effectively.

  • On-site training: We come to your location
  • Workshops & Seminars: Intensive short-term training

Our Services

Information about the services we provide

What types of services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of agricultural services:

  • Agricultural Services: Poultry & livestock training, mechanized farming, fertilizer production
  • Consultancy & Training: Agricultural consultancy, skills acquisition, seminars & workshops
  • Commercial & Trading: Supply of raw materials, agricultural equipment dealership
  • Industrial & Manufacturing: Production of agricultural machines
  • Transport & Logistics: Transport services, logistics, vehicle hiring

Yes, we offer customized services tailored to the specific needs of businesses, including:
